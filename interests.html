Codu Community

Codu Community is a space for developers to meet up and get to know each other in Dublin, Ireland. It great for those new to developing to meet others in the field as well as those who are in the same position as them. Created and organised by Niall Maher, meetups are welcome to all, no matter your experience and expertise, and are a great place to network. I have attended meetups here where I was lucky to meet many people interested in tech at various stages in their career and I look forward to attending future meet ups. Codu Community
